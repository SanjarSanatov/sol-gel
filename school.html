School open for

senior students, postgraduate students or young scientists up to the 35 years old

30 April 2020

Samarkand city (Uzbekistan)

In the framework of the Sixth International Conference of CIS Countries «Sol-gel synthesis and research of inorganic compounds, hybrid functional materials and disperse systems» - "SOL-GEL 2020" will be organized an International Young Scientific School “New frontiers in Sol-Gel Materials”.

The International Young Scientists School is expected to involve young scientists from different countries, as well as lecturers. Lectures will be carried out by the leading world scientists in the field of sol-gel science and technology from Russia, near and far abroad. Oral reports of young scientists and a special poster session with the active participation of young scientists are also planned. The best oral and the best poster presentation will be awarded with certificates and memorable prizes. Abstracts will be published.

The aim of the Scientific School is to familiarize undergraduate and graduate students and young scientists with modern works in the field of sol-gel technology. Nowadays, sol-gel science and technology has become an extraordinarily multidisciplinary area of research, spanning chemistry, physics, biology, biotechnology, biochemistry, electronics, and related engineering branches, materials science and nanotechnology. Based on this, the range of application of these technologies is very wide.

International Young Scientists School!

We would like to invite all interested young scientists, especially undergraduate and graduate students or young scientists under 35 years old, whose scientific and industrial activities related to sol-gel technology, to register to our event - The International School of Young Scientists “New Frontiers in Sol-Gel Materials”.

LEARN MORE

The venue of the School is Samarkand State University (SamSU), at the address: Samarkand city, University Boulevard, 15.

The School program is based on the wishes of the participants. You can send your suggestions to the Organizing Committee at info@solgel2020.uz.

Please note that the International Young Scientists School is not a state educational program or a refresher course.

To participate in the School, it is necessary to register before April 30 and attach abstracts to the conference e-mail: info@solgel2020.uz. It is possible to participate in the school with an oral or poster presentation or as an auditor.

English and Russian will be School working languages. Participation in the International School of Young Scientists is not obligatory for the conference participants. If you have any questions, write to: info@solgel2020.uz
