Information on the resumption of the preparation and holding of the Sixth International Conference of the CIS Countries in Samarkand “Sol-gel synthesis and research of inorganic compounds, hybrid functional materials and disperse systems” – “SOL-GEL 2021” will be additionally available on our website

CONFERENCE TOPICS

The theoretical aspects of sol-gel process

  • Sol-gel synthesis and research of nanoparticles
  • Sol-gel films, coatings and membranes
  • Hybrid inorganic and organic-inorganic materials
  • Nano- and micro-structured materials by sol-gel method
  • Porous materials and hierarchical structured materials
  • Characterization and modeling techniques for sol-gel materials
  • Sol-gel materials for environmental protection, agriculture and biomedicine applications
  • Applications of sol-gel science and technology
